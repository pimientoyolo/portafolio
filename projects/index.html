MiThermal – LWIR Hyperspectral Imaging Simulation

Simulation framework for the acquisition of long-wave infrared (LWIR) hyperspectral images in 3D environments, incorporating atmospheric absorption effects. The system leverages physically based rendering to realistically model thermal radiation and spectral behavior, enabling the generation of synthetic hyperspectral data for analysis and research in infrared imaging.

Malaria detection IA

Develop a deep learning-based system to detect malaria parasites in blood slide images, focusing on classifying infected and uninfected cells at the trophozoite stage.

Universegame – E-Commerce Web System

Universegame is a web-based system designed for product and sales management. The platform includes an online store that allows users to browse products, add items to a shopping cart, and complete purchases, while administrators can manage inventory and generate sales reports. The project also includes comprehensive documentation, database diagrams, activity diagrams, and a sprint-based development timeline.

Finanzify – Personal Finance Management Platform

Personal finance management platform that allows users to easily track income and expenses by category, monitor investments, create budgets, and receive personalized financial recommendations. The system provides clear and concise data visualization through interactive charts, helping users better understand and manage their financial situation
